audio transcripts in the browser

with this little tool, you can "upload" an audio file and write a transcript of it in your browser without having to install anything.

everything will happen on your own computer, so you don't have to worry about privacy. hope you find it useful!

  • § plays/pauses
  • TAB & SHIFT-TAB skips backward/forward 5 seconds
  • ALT- & ALT+ sets speed (1.0x)
